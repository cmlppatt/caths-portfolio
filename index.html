CATH

I am a Franco-Ontarian developper & designer working at the intersection of code, design and storytelling.

Websites shouldn't be boring. I love to play with bold, expressive and interactive visual styles. Whether I am writing code or sketching layouts, I bring a curious, experimental mindset and strive to make digital spaces more engaging, memorable and fun!

In my spare time, for the past 12 years I have been playing hockey for my hometown team, the Aurora Panthers. I love the high energy and teamwork of the game. No, I am not a Leafs fan… Go Oilers!

Every designer has a killer playlist. I am a huge country music fan so I always have some on! But, when in a crunch, I pull out my secret weapon to get the creative juices flowing: French Canadian music.

I am a candidate for the bachelor of Global Business and Digital Arts with honours at the University of Waterloo and am 1 credit away from completing the computing minor.
