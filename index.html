CATH

I am a Franco-Ontarian developper & designer working at the intersection of code, design and storytelling.

Websites shouldn't be boring! I love to play with bold, expressive and interactive visual styles. I bring a curious and intentional mindset to web design, exploring how visuals, interaction and code can work together to create websites that are immersive and fun.

When I'm not working on creative projects, I'm probably on the ice. I’ve spent 12 years growing as a hockey player with the Aurora Panthers. No I am not a Leafs fan... Go Oilers!!

Every designer has a killer playlist. I am a huge country music fan so I always have some on! But, when in a crunch, I pull out my secret weapon to get the creative juices flowing: French Canadian music.

Candidate for the bachelor of Global Business and Digital Arts with honours at the University of Waterloo and completing the computing minor.
